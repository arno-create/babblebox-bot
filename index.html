Daily Play

Babblebox Daily is the low-player-count habit layer: shared, quick, and light on storage.

Babblebox Daily

Daily Shuffle

One deterministic word shuffle per UTC day. Everyone gets the same puzzle. Results stay compact: attempts, solved/failed, and solve time.

  • 1-3 minute puzzle flow
  • 3 attempts per day
  • shareable text result output
  • streaks, clears, and leaderboard support
Storage Discipline

Built for small databases

Babblebox does not store the puzzle payload itself if it can regenerate it from the date. Raw Daily rows are bounded and pruned. Long-term streaks and totals live in the profile row.

  • one result row per user per day
  • no media or blob generation
  • no reroll farming
  • raw Daily rows pruned after 180 days

Buddy / Profile / Vault

Identity without turning Babblebox into an economy clone.

Babblebox Buddy

Every user gets one lightweight companion with a species, nickname, style, mood, level, title slot, and featured badge. Cosmetics come from local code-defined catalogs, not stored assets.

Profile and Vault

`/profile` and `/vault` pull together Buddy status, Daily streaks, utility summaries, and multiplayer highlights into one compact personal overview that stays useful even for lower-activity users.

Privacy, Reliability, and Support

Babblebox tries to stay useful without collecting more than it needs.

Data handling

  • Gameplay state is primarily temporary session state.
  • Utility and profile persistence stay compact and bounded.
  • No screenshot storage, image blobs, or full watched-message archives.
  • Capture transcripts are delivered privately and not kept as a long-term archive.
  • Daily challenge payloads regenerate from deterministic local data instead of being stored each day.

Support and community

Babblebox lives alongside the inevitable friendship support server. That is the best place to test updates, report issues, share screenshots, and shape future Daily modes or Buddy polish.
