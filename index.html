For server owners

If you need moderation help, Babblebox keeps it compact.

Shield is optional, conservative, and admin-facing. It sits alongside compact admin lifecycle helpers for follow-up roles and verification cleanup, not as a sprawling moderation suite.

It covers likely privacy leaks, promo or invite spam, and scam or malicious-link bait. AI assist stays off by default, only reviews messages local Shield already flagged, and is currently limited to the support server.
